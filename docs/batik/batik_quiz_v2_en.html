About Ansari

Ansari is an AI assistant designed specifically for Islamic information and knowledge. It provides accurate, authentic information about Islam while citing its sources, helping users access reliable Islamic knowledge 24/7.

About BATIK

BATIK (Broad Automated Test of Islamic Knowledge) is a comprehensive test of Islamic knowledge developed to evaluate AI systems. It covers 100 multiple-choice questions across diverse categories including Islamic theology, jurisprudence, history, and practice.

Ansari's Performance

On the BATIK benchmark, Ansari achieved 100% accuracy across all 100 questions. Additionally, 65% of its answers included citations to Islamic sources.

How This Quiz Works

Can you beat Ansari on an Islamic Quiz?

Accuracy has always been a key focus of our efforts on Ansari. In preparation for Ansari v3, we evaluated the BATIK (Basic Automated Test of Islamic Knowledge) dataset on it. BATIK consists of 100 multiple choice questions. Ansari scored 100%. More impressively, however, 65% of its answers included references to Qur'an, Hadith, or highly respected Islamic Encyclopedias. More details here including detailed answers from Ansari.

To help promote Ansari v3 and show what AI is capable of, we also have an interactive quiz where people can pit themselves against the AI and see Ansari's actual answers. When you do the quiz, it will give you an image that you can post on social media. Let us know what your score is!

Behind the Scenes

For those who are curious about how Ansari works, we wanted to take you behind the scenes. Feel free to skip this section if you're not interested.

While some of the improvements are user visible, many of Ansari v3's most significant improvements are in the underlying technology. A reminder that we are open source, and that both the frontend and the backend are available as open source on GitHub.

  • MongoDB Implementation: Moved from SQL to MongoDB for better handling of complex messaging
  • Enhanced Search Tools: Added Tafsir Encyclopedia and Usul.ai integration
  • React Native Framework: Rebuilt interface for cross-platform support
  • Improved CI/CD Pipeline: Automated testing and deployment processes for faster, more reliable releases
  • Containerized Infrastructure: Docker implementation for consistent development and production environments
